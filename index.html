About Bantayan Island

Bantayan Island is a tropical paradise located in the Visayan Sea, Philippines. With its powdery white sand beaches, crystal clear turquoise waters, and lush tropical landscapes, Bantayan Island is a haven for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure.

Discover the island's rich marine biodiversity by snorkeling or diving in its vibrant coral reefs. Explore the charming coastal villages and immerse yourself in the warm hospitality of the locals. Indulge in delicious fresh seafood and tropical fruits, or simply unwind on the beach and soak up the sun.

Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or an adventure-filled holiday, Bantayan Island offers something for everyone.
